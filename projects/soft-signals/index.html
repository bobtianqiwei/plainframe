Soft Signals

Interface studies for a messaging product that wanted to feel quieter, slower, and more intentional than the category norm.

Avery Lin
Product design / Motion direction
2026

Context

Soft Signals started from a simple idea: many communication products are efficient but emotionally exhausting.

The work explored how motion, feedback timing, and pacing could support a calmer interaction tone.

Design Move

The interface system reduces visual noise and uses animation more like punctuation than decoration.

That is part of why Plainframe fits the project: the template is designed to describe decisions clearly without adding extra visual clutter.
