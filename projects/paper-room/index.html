Paper Room

An editorial installation where spreads, captions, and page rhythm become the architecture of the room itself.

Avery Lin
Editorial design / Installation
2025

Context

Paper Room was designed as a hybrid between publication and exhibition. Instead of treating the book as a separate artifact, the room became an extension of the sequencing logic.

The work moves between editorial detail and spatial framing.

Design Move

The visual language stays reduced so that scale shifts, paper textures, and caption rhythm can carry the experience.

Plainframe supports this kind of project by keeping the page itself understated and letting the argument live in the images and the writing.
