North Window

A gallery graphics system where text, scale, and circulation work together to control pace and attention.

Avery Lin
Spatial graphics / Exhibition design
2024

Context

North Window was developed for a small exhibition with a limited palette and a strong architectural character.

The visual system needed to feel present without overwhelming the objects or the room.

Design Move

Typography and placement did most of the work. The graphics stayed quiet so the sequence of attention could stay focused on movement, objects, and reading distance.

This project demonstrates how Plainframe handles work that is less screen-based but still benefits from a clean project write-up.
