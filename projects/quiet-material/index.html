Quiet Material

A visual system that gives technical material research a softer and more legible public voice.

Avery Lin
Brand system / Editorial direction
2026

Context

Quiet Material began as a visual identity for a studio working across biomaterials, color studies, and fabrication research.

The challenge was to keep the system calm and precise without making it feel sterile.

Design Move

The identity uses a narrow tonal range, generous spacing, and a flexible text-first layout so writing and imagery can alternate without competing.

That makes the project a good demo for Plainframe because the page needs to carry both visual work and a short editorial explanation.
